Will you be observing Ramadan?

Are you concerned about exam dates clashing with Ramadan?

If you will be observing Ramadan, or other religious festivals and holy days, which may clash with the scheduled examination period, you can complete a Religious Observance and examinations form, and your School will then do what they can to accommodate your needs and make reasonable adjustments.

The Religious Observance and examinations form is available via studentcentral or here. It is also available in hard copy from your School and Students' Union Offices.

The deadline for submitting the form for summer examinations is 31 January 2018.

Further information about completing the form can be found in the University's 'Religious observance and examination arrangements' Policy. You can also contact the Student Experience and Equalities Manager at equality@brighton.ac.uk if you would like further guidance. Furthermore, the University's Chaplaincy and Students' Union can advise you on how best to manage your religious commitments during examination periods.

The following websites provide some useful guidance related to how to manage examinations during Ramadan:

http://productivemuslim.com/exams-and-ramadan/

https://www.nhs.uk/Livewell/HealthyRamadan/Pages/healthyramadanhome.aspx

Travel Smart to campus

Win prizes to celebrate the re-launch of Travel Smart

The Travel Smart platform is a great and simple way to find other students or staff to share your car journey into university with, saving you money, reducing pollution and congestion.

Register your journey, save money & win prizes!

To celebrate the re-launch of the platform, we're giving you the chance to win one of three prizes of 50 quid to put towards car-sharing, and one of 100 bags of Divine chocolate coins - a tasty reminder of how car-sharing can save you money!

To be in with a chance of winning, all you need to do is sign up to Travel Smart (if you're not already) and register a journey, in three easy steps:

Visit the Travel Smart website here
Join the car-sharing community by clicking 'get started', or login if you're already a member
Add your regular or one-off journey
The first 100 people to add a journey will win a bag of Divine dark chocolate coins. Everyone* who has a journey registered on Travel Smart by 1 March 2018 will be entered into a prize draw to win 50 quid to put towards car-sharing!

Please ensure you read the competition terms and conditions here.

*This does include those already signed up to Travel Smart.

Why use Travel Smart?

Using Travel Smart can help reduce your driving costs you can register any journey on the platform that you'd like to share, whether that's to class, between campuses or taking a trip.
Once your journey is registered, you will get email updates of potential car-shares
You are able to specify if you're a student or member of staff
We are part of the East Sussex County Council (ESCC) umbrella scheme, so you can find car-share partners from across ESCC as well as the University of Brighton
You can communicate with others and arrange car sharing via the Liftshare App.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the Travel Smart platform, or the competition, please get in touch with the Environment Team via c-change@brighton.ac.uk

Weather update: Tuesday 27 Feb, 3pm

*Tuesday 27 Feb: Weather update for all students*

There has been continuing snowfall across all campuses, but all buildings remain open.

Wherever possible, we will continue to ensure that there is no disruption to services provided, but this may change dependent upon the severity of the weather conditions. Please refer to your university email account and the University's weather blog (below) for updates and details of any adjustments to services or building opening hours on your campus.

There has been minor disruption to travel routes and public transport, so please do continue to plan your journeys ahead. Check your local transport provider for more information.

Brighton campuses
The UoB1 bus route is operating between all sites, although at a reduced speed, and Brighton bus services have resumed normal service.

Stanford Medical Centre, Brighton
Please note that if weather becomes severe, the medical centre may be subject to closure. If this is the case, patients will be contacted by telephone providing their contact details are up to date.

Additionally, appointments will be available at 175 Preston Road and we will also be offering telephone appointments.

Contact details for the centre can be found here if you have any queries or concerns.
